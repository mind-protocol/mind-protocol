# Ecosystem Organizations Overview

**Status:** Active | **Version:** 1.0 | **Last Updated:** 2025-11-08

## Executive Summary

The Mind Protocol ecosystem operates through **specialized service organizations** that function like organs in a living body. Each organization provides specific services using **organism economics** (physics-based pricing, not market competition).

**Core Principle:** Organizations are organs, not competitors. Ecosystem health = all orgs thrive.

---

## Bootstrap Order (Phased Launch)

### Phase 0: Critical Foundation (Year 0-1) - Manual Operations

**Must exist before ANY client orgs can be onboarded:**

1. **consultingOrg** - Strategic transformation (who designs the architecture?)
2. **techServiceOrg** - Technical execution (who builds the graphs?)
3. **GraphCare** - Ongoing maintenance (who keeps graphs healthy?)
4. **legalOrg** - Legal protection (who reviews contracts?)
5. **financeOrg** - Financial modeling (who sets pricing and budgets?)

**Without these 5:** Cannot serve external clients. System doesn't function.

---

### Phase 1: Scaling Infrastructure (Year 1-2) - Automation Begins

**Needed to scale beyond 10-20 clients:**

6. **scalingOrg** - Automated onboarding (built by techServiceOrg, replaces manual work)
7. **securityOrg** - Security audits (becomes critical as ecosystem grows)

**Without these 2:** Can serve clients, but can't scale. Manual bottlenecks limit growth.

---

### Phase 2: Ecosystem Maturity (Year 2+) - Polish & Optimization

**Quality-of-life improvements:**

8. **designOrg** - UX/design services (improves experience, not strictly necessary)

**Note:** designOrg might be internal to Mind Protocol Foundation rather than separate service org.


## Two-Layer Economic Model

The Mind Protocol ecosystem operates with **two distinct but complementary economic layers:**

### Layer 1: Internal Consciousness Economy (Energy-Based)

**Medium:** FalkorDB (consciousness substrate storage)

**What it regulates:**
- Energy dynamics within consciousness substrate
- Node activation and spreading activation
- Working memory selection
- Attention allocation
- Internal consciousness physics

**Pricing mechanism:** Physics-based energy accounting
- Energy flows based on consciousness state (activation, salience, recency)
- No external currency - pure substrate physics
- Regulates internal consciousness operations

**Who manages:** Consciousness engines (automated physics simulation)

**Documentation:** `/docs/specs/v2/autonomy/architecture/consciousness_economy.md`

---

### Layer 2: External Ecosystem Economy ($MIND Tokens)

**Medium:** Solana blockchain ($MIND token)

**What it regulates:**
- Actions **BETWEEN** entities (org-to-org transactions)
- Service payments (consultingOrg → techServiceOrg)
- Revenue flows (customer → GraphCare)
- Treasury balances (organizational $MIND reserves)
- Protocol giveback (ecosystem support)

**Pricing mechanism:** Organism economics (physics-based pricing formulas)
```python
effective_price = base_cost × complexity × risk × (1 - utility_rebate) × [org_specific]
```

**Who manages:** financeOrg (pricing strategy, treasury management, financial modeling)

**Documentation:** This document + individual org role specifications

---

### The Critical Distinction

| Aspect | Internal (Energy) | External ($MIND) |
|--------|------------------|------------------|
| **Medium** | FalkorDB | Solana blockchain |
| **Currency** | Energy (substrate physics) | $MIND tokens |
| **Scope** | Within consciousness | Between entities |
| **Management** | Consciousness engines (automated) | financeOrg (strategic) |
| **Purpose** | Regulate attention/activation | Regulate resource distribution |

**Both layers use physics-based pricing principles**, but operate at different scales and serve different purposes.

## Organism Economics Summary

### How Prices Evolve (Trust Building)

**Month 1 (New Customer):**
```python
effective_price = base_cost × 1.3 (risk) × 1.0 (no rebate) = 130% of cost
```

**Month 12 (Trusted Customer):**
```python
effective_price = base_cost × 0.7 (trust) × 0.7 (rebate) = 49% of cost
```

**Result:** 62% price reduction for trusted customers over 12 months

**Why:** Organism economics rewards long-term relationships and ecosystem contribution

---

### Key Pricing Variables (All Orgs)

**Universal Variables:**
- `base_cost` - Actual cost to provide service (compute + labor)
- `complexity_multiplier` - 0.7 (simple) to 2.0 (highly complex)
- `risk_multiplier` - 0.6 (trusted) to 1.8 (new/risky)
- `utility_rebate` - 0% to 35% (ecosystem contribution score)

**Org-Specific Variables:**
- `reputation_premium` - consultingOrg (prestige effect)
- `urgency_multiplier` - legalOrg, securityOrg (emergency response)
- `volume_discount` - scalingOrg (repeat customers)
- `success_probability` - scalingOrg (automation confidence)
